About Me

I have really enjoyed relearning HTML (I actually remember quite a bit from working on 1st wave iMacs in middle school) and CSS is very straight foward. I enjoy JavaScript the most so far, both in what it does for websites and the challenge of loops and iterators (I really liked my Logic and Proofs course in college.

I think I bring good sense of graphic design and a contempary artisic eye to any project, along with a working class drive to get results and accomplish goals on schedule. As I develop my toolbox of skills with frontend and backend engineering, I feel I will be a great employee for a number of coding companies.

In my freetime I enjoy swimming at my local YWCA, taking in art exhibits at The Walker or MIA, and watching odd and critically acclaimed films at home or the theater.
