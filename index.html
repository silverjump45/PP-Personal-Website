About Me

Howdy! I'm Walter Leinen, a former electrical apprentice and sometime printmaker, who is currently studying to be a Full Stack Engineer. I hope to complete my certification by the end of September 2024 and enter the Coding Job Market.

Update: My studies are progressing well, with Test-Driven Develop as my most favorite course so far. Currently I'm taking my time and carefully learning React and hooks. I'm looking forward to putting my skills together and coding my first App soon.

I like to think I bring good sense of graphic design and a contemporary artistic eye to any project, along with a working class drive to get results and accomplish goals on schedule. As I develop my toolbox of skills with Frontend and Backend engineering, I feel I will be a great employee for a lot of coding companies.

In my freetime I enjoy swimming at my local YWCA, taking in art exhibits at The Walker or MIA, catching a baseball game on the radio, tv or in person, and watching odd and critically acclaimed films at home or the theater.
